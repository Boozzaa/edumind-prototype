What is “IT” really?

Instead of buzzwords, we break IT down into a handful of components you can remember: devices, networks, servers, cloud & apps that sit on top.

4:12 min Beginner No technical jargon

Key points

  • IT is just a set of layers that move and transform data.
  • Each layer has its own role: device, network, server, app.
  • Most business problems are not “magic IT” – but misaligned layers.
